## Classify The Failure

- No germination
- Uneven germination
- Emerged, then died
- Leggy or weak growth
- Disease or physical damage
- Record too poor to know
- Seedlings healthy, but plan changed

## Drafting Constraint

Do not make the tray symbolize resilience. Identify which variable can be
changed next time and which information was never collected. A failed crop and
a failed record are different results.
