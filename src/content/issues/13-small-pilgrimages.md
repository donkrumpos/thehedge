## Drafting Questions

Write 500-800 words from the record.

1. Did a stated purpose change the quality of the walk?
2. Did calling it a pilgrimage clarify the action or decorate it?
3. What remained completely ordinary?
4. What did the route demand from the body?

End at the point of return. Do not manufacture a revelation at the
destination.
