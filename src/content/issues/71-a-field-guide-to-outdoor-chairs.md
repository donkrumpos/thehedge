**Status:** Requires observed use, not staged photographs.

For each chair or seating position record:

- Time and duration occupied
- Sun, shade, wind, temperature
- Surface under chair
- View from the seat
- Distance and angle to another chair
- Distance to door, path, table, or work
- Whether the chair was moved
- What ended the sitting

Classify only after observation: work chair, conversation chair, waiting chair,
watching chair, eating chair, decorative chair, or chair nobody chooses.

The final piece should explain placement through use. Do not infer a person's
private mood from posture or duration.
